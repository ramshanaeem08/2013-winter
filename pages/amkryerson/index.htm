About Me:

I started to edit in this Wiki, when I was fed up with my husband's whining that he had found a mistake or that something was missing. He is too lazy to do it himself, so I ended up doing it. Nowadays, he 'collects' any problems he finds and then he gives them to me for fixing ... go figure. However, I am German and I used to live in the UK, so please excuse any spelling / grammar mistakes.

So far, I have four toons on Ghallanda and four on Cannith. Ghallanda is our 'main' gaming server, whereas I use the Cannith server to try out new builds / races / classes. We are a tiny guild - only 5 members - and now that we are spread across 6 timezones, we have less chances to play together - sigh. For more info, check my player profile on the DDO site.

At the moment, I am on hiatus - after quitting my job last year, I have gone back to full-time education - so there is not enough time to game / update the Wiki AND study. But I will be back.
